    History

    BookStore was founded in October 2007 by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal, alumni of the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi and former Amazon employees. The company initially focused on online book sales with country-wide shipping. Later they added used book selling service through which customer could sell their old books online after that they also started selling old books at low cost. BookStore slowly grew in prominence and was receiving 100 orders per day by 2008. In 2010, BookStore acquired the Bangalore-based social book discovery service WeRead from Lulu.com.
